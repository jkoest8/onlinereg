Library eCard Application Online Version

Those who live within the SELCO area can apply online to get an eCard. If you already have a library card registered with Rochester Public Library, you can continue to use it and do not need to apply for an e-card.

Rochester Public Library eCards:

  • Provide full access to the libraryâ€™s online resources, including eBooks and other downloads.
  • Allow you to check out items from RPL's BookBoxes.
  • Do not allow you to place holds or check out physical materials.

You can get a library card with full borrowing privileges by stopping in at the Rochester Public Library and speaking with someone at the Public Service Desk.

Note: Your eCard will be deleted if you live outside the SELCO area.
